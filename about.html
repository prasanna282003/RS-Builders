Our Story

Founded in 2010, RS Interlock Brick Builders began as a small family-owned business in Shivamogga with a vision to revolutionize construction materials in the region. What started as a modest operation with just 5 employees has now grown into one of the most trusted names in interlock brick manufacturing.

Over the years, we've invested in modern technology and trained our workforce to produce bricks that meet international quality standards while being perfectly suited for local construction needs. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.
